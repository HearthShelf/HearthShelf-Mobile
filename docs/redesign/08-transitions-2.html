One lift, three doses — final values

Consensus reached across 08 (options + fold analysis + cross-review) and 09/09.2 (route semantics, entry-as-promise, dose model, no toast). This page is the acceptance record: the full contract runs live below, every claim is audited, and the implementation is mapped to real files.

Micro · tab swaps
8px · 160ms
Home ↔ Library ↔ Stats ↔ Playing ↔ More. Frame, tab bar, and mini dock never move — only the content layer lifts.
Standard · pushes, pops, player
18px · 240ms
Book, series, club, settings, search (pushed), Now Playing from the mini dock, fold right-leaf swaps. Parent settles to 98.5% + dims. Pop sinks in ~210ms. Enter cubic-bezier(.16,1,.3,1), exit cubic-bezier(.5,0,.75,.5).
Zero · fallback
0px · 120ms fade
OS Reduce Motion, tabletop posture (horizontal hinge cancels vertical travel), low-power. Navigation is still acknowledged; nothing displaces.

On-device QA script (sign-off gate)

  • Push/pop parity — open a book from Home, Library, Search, and a club; every push and pop must look identical on iOS and Android.
  • Player entrance — from mini dock and from the Now tab: no full-height bottom slide anywhere; mini dock hides only while the player owns the screen.
  • Swipe rejection — drag down hard and slow on the player: max ~14px travel, springs back, no dismissal, no toast; iOS edge swipe and full-screen swipe both dead; Android back pops to the previous screen with the mini dock visible.
  • Tab feel — rapid tab cycling must never queue or stutter (interrupt = start the new micro lift immediately).
  • Reduced motion — enable OS setting: every transition becomes a 120ms fade, including the rubber band (which reduces to no displacement).
  • Pop symmetry — push 3 levels deep (club → book → series) and pop out: parent luminance must land exactly where it started.
