One grammar, deliberately dosed

One lift.
Three doses.

Claude’s Shelf Lift has the better foundation. My revision makes the rule more literal: every new surface arrives with the same fade-and-lift gesture, but the distance is scaled to the relationship. The player gets the standard lift—not a sheet-like rise from the bottom edge.

Entry motion is a promise. Nothing enters like a dismissible sheet unless it can actually be dismissed like one. Now Playing lifts into focus, stays anchored, and resists downward drag without explaining itself in a toast.

The motion contract

Same gesture. Different volume.

The consistency comes from the shape, not identical distance everywhere. Every entrance fades from zero while translating upward into rest; every exit sinks and fades. Only the dose changes. This keeps the system recognizable without making a five-item tab bar feel like five modal presentations.

Micro · 8px · 160ms

Tabs whisper.

The frame, tab bar, and mini-player remain fixed. Only the destination content lifts a short distance.

  • Home ↔ Library ↔ Stats
  • Playing and More tabs
  • No directional left/right travel
Standard · 18px · 240ms

Routes speak.

A parent settles to 98.5% and dims while the new surface lifts into focus. The player uses this exact dose.

  • Book, series, club, settings
  • Search as a pushed route
  • Player and fold-pane swaps
Zero · 0px · 120ms

Fallback stays legible.

When displacement is wrong, the same opacity handoff remains. It still acknowledges navigation without moving content.

  • OS Reduce Motion
  • Horizontal tabletop hinge
  • Low-power safety fallback
Posture proof

The hinge stays quiet.

In book posture, the standard 18px lift runs parallel to the vertical hinge and is clipped to the right leaf. In tabletop posture the hinge is horizontal, so vertical translation drops to zero and the pane uses the fallback fade.

Route doctrine

Make the rule hard to misuse.

The implementation should choose a dose from route semantics, not screen names scattered across the app. This also makes the Search correction explicit: it is pushed from a search affordance; it is not a sixth or substituted tab.

Downward gesture

Resistance is the response.

The player follows a downward drag at roughly 10% of finger travel, caps near 14px, then springs to rest in about 260ms. It does not navigate, collapse, or display explanatory UI.
