Doctrine — the in-house addendum

Two rules worth keeping — with restraint

V3 keeps an accessibility floor and reach evidence, but now starts from implemented beta behavior. Existing safeguards and completed screens are marked as current product, not redesign inventions.

D-REACH · extends D-THUMB

Prove the thumb zone, don't assert it

Every primary action must sit inside the bottom ~55% one-handed arc on a 6.7" phone, shown with a reach overlay. Destructive or irreversible actions sit OUTSIDE the fast-tap zone so they can't be fired by a stray thumb. "Reachable" is a measurement, not a claim.

D-A11Y · new

An accessibility & theme floor on every surface

Signed-in surfaces retain theme parity; the branded pre-auth surface guarantees contrast. Core reading and playback layouts reflow for large text without clipping. Every icon-only control has a label for VoiceOver/TalkBack, in a logical focus order. EmberBurst, CoverGlow, and carousel parallax all honor Reduce Motion with a static fallback. Color never carries meaning alone (sync state gets an icon shape, not just red/green).
